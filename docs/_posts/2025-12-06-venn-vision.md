## Highlighted Tracks

### Track 1: "Serres Climbing"

**Why this matters**: It shows the source material in its most direct form, creating a reference point for how later tracks transform the same content through different lenses.

### Track 3: "Serres Method"

**Why this matters**: It demonstrates how a Luhmann systems perspective transforms lived experience into operational communication, showing the stark contrast between phenomenological and systems-theoretic approaches.
