Welcome to **Hyperframes** — a new public portal for ongoing research in sociological worlds, attention architectures, and generative narrative variations.

## What's Live Now

### Research Areas
Conceptual introductions to:
- **Sociological & Phenomenological Worlds**: Framework for analyzing 105+ contemporary and historical worlds through Schutz, Bourdieu, and Luhmann
- **Attention Architecture**: Meta-cognitive systems for understanding attention
- **Braitenberg Vehicles**: Synthetic psychology and diagrammatic research
- **Intervention Techniques**: Methods for generating world and narrative variations

### Audiobook Projects
Three released projects:
- **Variations on Reading** — 10 philosophical variations with Blues Rock, Psychedelic Soul, and Slowcore musical aesthetics
- **NYC Subway Commuter (Musique Concrète)** — Urban phenomenology with processed subway sounds
- **NYC Subway Commuter (A Cappella)** — Same narrative with human vocal textures
- **Tea Ceremony Variations** — Ritual reimagined through multiple philosophical lenses

## Repository Access

All research materials, workflows, and audiobook production tools are available in the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/mostlycached/schema). See the [Repository Guide](/guide) for how to use the variational techniques and workflows yourself.

Stay tuned for updates!
