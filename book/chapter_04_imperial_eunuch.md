# The Imperial Eunuch

This room is a Loop. The Eunuch stands at the Red Gate because the logic of his existence is the threshold. He cannot go in, and he cannot go back. He is the Zeno's Arrow of the Empire, frozen in the millisecond before arrival.

The Center must remain undefined. The Emperor exists only to be *feared*. If the door were to open, the tension would collapse, and the room would dissolve. Therefore, the door remains locked. The bronze tortoise lock keeps the Mystery in.

There is only the Gate, the Paper, the Stamp, and the Silence. He is trapped in a machine that produces nothing but its own continuation. He waits for the realization that the inside and the outside are identical.
