# Optimization: The Aghori Sect

**World**: The Aghori Sect (#88)
**Type**: Long-lasting (Genealogy: Kapalika -> Bhakti -> Varanasi)

## 1. Value Systems Selection (from VALUES.md)

To optimize this world, we focus on:

*   **Nietzschean Critique (Values)**: The Aghori is the ultimate "Overman" who has transcended the binary of Good and Evil. He operates "Beyond Good and Evil," transvaluating the values of purity and pollution.
*   **Psychoanalytic & Post-Structuralist (Values)**: The concept of the **Abject** (Julia Kristeva). The Aghori embraces what society rejects (corpses, feces, decay) to collapse the boundary between the Self and the Other.
*   **Epistemic Truth (Epistemic)**: Radical **Non-Duality** (*Advaita*). If God is everywhere, God is in the filth. To reject the filth is to reject God.

## 2. Intervention Techniques (from INTERVENTION_TECHNIQUES.md)

We apply these techniques to shift the world and reveal its core:

*   **Defamiliarization (Ostranenie)**:
    *   *The Skull Bowl (Kapala)*: Eating from a human skull forces the mind to break its association between "death" and "terror" and replace it with "death" and "sustenance."
    *   *The Corpse as Altar*: Meditating on a dead body (*Shava Sadhana*) turns the ultimate taboo into a platform for enlightenment.

*   **Somatic Intervention**:
    *   *Bhasma (Corpse Ash)*: Smearing the body with ash from the cremation ground. It is a "second skin" of death, a constant reminder of mortality and the ultimate equality of all matter.

*   **Dialectics**:
    *   *Synthesis of Opposites*: The Aghori lives in the cremation ground (the end of life) to find immortality (the beginning of life). He eats the impure to become pure.

## 3. Registers

### Formal Register
The Aghori Sect functions as a "sociological stress test" for the binary codes of the caste system (Pure/Impure). By systematically violating taboos, the Aghori agent deconstructs the social construction of reality. The *Kapala* serves as a "negative feedback loop" dampener, neutralizing the revulsion response and allowing the agent to access a state of non-dual awareness (*Advaita*). The system optimizes for **Fearlessness** (*Abhaya*) by exposing the agent to maximum existential risk (disease, social ostracization, madness).
