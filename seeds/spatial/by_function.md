# Spatial Seeds: By Function

> What does the space enable?

---

## Work Space

Production, labor. Where things get made.

### Knowledge Work
- **Home office** — Residential work
- **Co-working space** — Shared work
- **Library** — Quiet work
- **Coffee shop** — Ambient work

### Creative Work
- **Art studio** — Visual production
- **Music studio** — Audio production
- **Writing room** — Text production
- **Workshop** — Craft production

### Physical Work
- **Factory** — Manufacturing
- **Farm** — Agriculture
- **Construction site** — Building
- **Kitchen** — Food production

---

## Rest Space

Recovery, sleep. Where bodies restore.

### Sleep Spaces
- **Bedroom** — Primary sleep
- **Guest room** — Visitor sleep
- **Napping spots** — Brief rest

### Recovery Spaces
- **Spa** — Deep relaxation
- **Sauna** — Heat recovery
- **Meditation room** — Mental rest
- **Garden** — Restorative contact

---

## Social Space

Encounter, assembly. Where people meet.

### Intimate Social
- **Living room** — Family/friend gathering
- **Kitchen** — Informal connection
- **Porch** — Threshold visiting

### Public Social
- **Bars/cafes** — Drinking/eating social
- **Parks** — Outdoor gathering
- **Plazas** — Urban commons

### Formal Social
- **Meeting rooms** — Structured assembly
- **Classrooms** — Educational gathering
- **Courtrooms** — Legal assembly

---

## Transit Space

Movement, passing through. Space as passage.

### Personal Transit
- **Hallways** — Building movement
- **Stairs** — Vertical movement
- **Elevators** — Mechanical vertical

### Public Transit
- **Streets** — Urban movement
- **Highways** — Long-distance car
- **Bus/train stations** — Mass transit nodes
- **Airports** — Air travel nodes

---

## Nature Space

Non-human encounter. Contact with other-than-human.

### Cultivated Nature
- **Gardens** — Tended plants
- **Parks** — Managed green
- **Farms** — Agricultural nature

### Wild Nature
- **Forests** — Tree ecosystems
- **Mountains** — Vertical wild
- **Ocean/lakes** — Water bodies
- **Desert** — Arid wild

---

## Sacred Space

Ritualized attention. Set apart.

### Religious
- **Churches** — Christian worship
- **Temples** — Hindu/Buddhist
- **Mosques** — Islamic prayer
- **Synagogues** — Jewish worship

### Personal Sacred
- **Home altar** — Private devotion
- **Meditation corner** — Practice space
- **Retreat centers** — Intensive practice

---

## Third Place

Neither home nor work. The other.

### Classic Third Places
- **Coffee shops** — Caffeine social
- **Bars/pubs** — Alcohol social
- **Barbershops** — Service + social

### Contemporary Third Places
- **Gyms** — Exercise + social
- **Libraries** — Learning + social
- **Makerspaces** — Making + social
- **Bookstores** — Browsing + social
