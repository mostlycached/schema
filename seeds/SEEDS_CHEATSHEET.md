## IX. WOUNDS — Ontological constraints

### Temporal
- **The Clock** (linear time) → Stifles *duration*
- **The Weekend** (production/recovery) → Stifles *rhythm*
- **The Deadline** → Stifles *unfolding*
- **The Career** → Stifles *metamorphosis*

### Spatial
- **Euclidean Space** → Stifles *felt space*
- **Private Property** → Stifles *commons*
- **Body-Boundary** → Stifles *extension*

### Ontological
- **The Individual** → Stifles *assemblage*
- **The Human** → Stifles *brotherhood with things*
- **The Metrics** → Stifles *qualitative*
- **The Language** → Stifles *ineffable*

> *Wounds are not healed. They are mapped. Knowing the wall lets you find the door.*
