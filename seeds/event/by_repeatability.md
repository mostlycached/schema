# Event Seeds: By Repeatability

> Can the event happen again? What is its temporal structure?

---

## One-Off Events

Singular, cannot be repeated.

### Life Events
- **Birth, death** — Each singular
- **First experiences** — First kiss, first book
- **Milestones** — Turning 18, 30, 50

### Unrepeatable Performances
- **Opening/closing nights** — First and last
- **Improvised performances** — Never the same twice
- **Living artist's final show**

### Threshold Ceremonies
- **Weddings** — Each union singular
- **Ordinations, graduations** — Transition marking
- **Funerals** — Unique farewell

---

## Recurring Events

Same event, regular schedule.

### Daily/Weekly
- **Morning practice** — Meditation, exercise
- **Sabbath** — Religious day of rest
- **Weekly meetings** — Classes, gatherings

### Monthly/Seasonal
- **Full moon gatherings** — Lunar marking
- **Solstice/equinox** — Solar marking
- **Holidays** — Cultural calendar

### Annual
- **Birthdays, anniversaries** — Personal marking
- **Annual conferences** — Professional gathering

---

## Serial Events

Connected events that build.

### Educational
- **Course sequences** — Prerequisites building
- **Degree programs** — Multi-year progression

### Creative
- **Album cycles** — Recording, touring, rest
- **Book series** — Multi-volume narrative

### Life Stages
- **Career stages** — Entry, growth, mastery
- **Parenting stages** — Infancy, childhood, adolescence

---

## Improvised Events

Emerges without planning.

### Emergent Social
- **Spontaneous gatherings** — "Let's all go..."
- **After-parties** — Unplanned continuation
- **Kitchen conversations** — Lingering

### Creative Emergence
- **Jam sessions** — Musicians finding each other
- **Generative conversations** — Ideas sparking

### Conversational Emergence
- **Deep talks** — Unexpected depth
- **Revelations** — Insight emerging

---

*Time structures experience differently. Know what kind of time the event lives in.*
