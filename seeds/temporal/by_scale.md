# Temporal Seeds: By Scale

> Rhythms, schedules, punctuations. From seconds to decades.

---

## Minute

Breath, pause, micro-ritual. The smallest units.

### Breath-Scale
- **Single breath** — 4-6 seconds
- **Breath cycle** — Inhalation + exhalation
- **Mindful pause** — One conscious breath

### Micro-Practices
- **Pause before response** — Reaction interruption
- **Standing up/sitting down** — Transition awareness
- **Before eating** — Brief gratitude
- **Doorway pauses** — Threshold marking

---

## Hour

Session, block, unit. The working increment.

### Focused Work
- **Pomodoro** — 25-minute sessions
- **Deep work blocks** — 90-minute sessions
- **Meeting hour** — Standard time unit
- **Class period** — 50-75 minutes

### Practice Sessions
- **Meditation sitting** — 20-60 minutes
- **Music practice** — Instrument time
- **Exercise session** — Gym/run/class
- **Therapy session** — 50 minutes

---

## Day

Daily practice, routine. The fundamental cycle.

### Wake Routines
- **Morning pages** — First writing
- **Morning practice** — Exercise/meditation
- **Breakfast ritual** — Day start
- **Intention setting** — Day framing

### Work Day
- **Core hours** — Primary work time
- **Breaks** — Pause rhythm
- **Lunch** — Mid-day refuel
- **Shutdown ritual** — Work ending

### Evening Routines
- **Dinner** — Evening meal
- **Evening practice** — Post-work activity
- **Wind-down** — Sleep preparation
- **Reflection** — Day review

---

## Week

Weekly rhythm, recurring day. Seven-day cycle.

### Weekly Structure
- **Weekdays** — Work days
- **Weekend** — Rest/other days
- **Sabbath** — Sacred rest day

### Weekly Recurrences
- **Weekly meeting** — Same time
- **Weekly class** — Recurring lesson
- **Weekly practice** — Regular activity
- **Weekly review** — GTD/reflection

---

## Month

Cycle, period. Lunar and calendar rhythms.

### Calendar Month
- **Monthly planning** — 30-day horizon
- **Monthly review** — Period reflection
- **Monthly bills** — Financial rhythm
- **Monthly meetings** — Less frequent gathering

### Lunar Month
- **Full moon** — Peak/completion
- **New moon** — Beginnings
- **Lunar phases** — Waxing/waning

---

## Season

Quarterly, agricultural. Nature's rhythm.

### Natural Seasons
- **Spring** — Emergence, beginnings
- **Summer** — Fullness, intensity
- **Fall** — Harvest, letting go
- **Winter** — Rest, dormancy

### Institutional Seasons
- **Academic terms** — Semester/quarter
- **Fiscal quarters** — Business rhythm
- **Sports seasons** — Athletic calendar

---

## Year

Anniversary, annual event. The great return.

### Annual Markers
- **Birthday** — Personal year
- **New Year** — Collective reset
- **Anniversary** — Relationship year
- **Holidays** — Cultural calendar

### Annual Rhythms
- **Tax season** — Financial obligation
- **Conference season** — Professional gathering
- **Vacation time** — Annual rest

---

## Life Stage

Decade, phase, era. The longest rhythms.

### Life Phases
- **Childhood** — Early formation
- **Adolescence** — Identity formation
- **Young adulthood** — Establishment
- **Middle age** — Mastery/crisis
- **Elder years** — Wisdom/decline

### Career Phases
- **Training** — Skill development
- **Establishment** — Building career
- **Mastery** — Peak competence
- **Mentoring** — Giving back
- **Retirement** — Post-career
