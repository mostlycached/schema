# Temporal Seeds: By Type

> What kind of time is it? What does this time do?

---

## Routine

Repeated, expected, stabilizing. The familiar return.

### Morning Routines
- **Wake time** — Consistent rising
- **Morning practice** — Exercise, meditation
- **Breakfast routine** — Eating rhythm
- **Commute** — Travel pattern

### Work Routines
- **Opening rituals** — Start of work
- **Email check** — Communication routine
- **Meeting cadence** — Regular assembly
- **Break rhythm** — Pause pattern

### Evening Routines
- **Dinner time** — Evening meal
- **Shutdown ritual** — Work ending
- **Evening activities** — Post-work routine
- **Sleep routine** — Bedtime ritual

### Weekly Routines
- **Cleaning day** — Maintenance routine
- **Shopping day** — Provision routine
- **Laundry day** — Care routine
- **Planning session** — Review routine

### Daily Rituals
- **Morning prayer** — Devotional opening
- **Meditation** — Contemplative practice
- **Grace** — Meal blessing
- **Evening prayer** — Day closing

### Life Transition Rituals
- **Birth ceremonies** — Welcome rituals
- **Coming of age** — Threshold rituals
- **Marriage** — Union rituals
- **Death rituals** — Farewell ceremonies

### Religious Rituals
- **Weekly services** — Sabbath worship
- **Holiday observance** — Calendar rituals
- **Pilgrimage** — Journey rituals
- **Rites of passage** — Initiation

### Secular Rituals
- **Birthday celebrations** — Annual personal
- **Graduation ceremonies** — Achievement marking
- **Award ceremonies** — Recognition rituals
- **Opening/closing ceremonies** — Event framing

### External Deadlines

- **Application deadlines** — Opportunity windows

### Self-Imposed Deadlines

- **Challenge deadlines** — 30-day projects
- **Commitment dates** — Promises made

### Competition Deadlines
- **Registration closes** — Entry windows
- **Submission deadlines** — Contest/grant dates

## Sabbath

Punctuation of non-work. Sacred pause.

### Religious Sabbath
- **Jewish Shabbat** — Friday sunset to Saturday sunset
- **Christian Sunday** — Day of rest
- **Islamic Jummah** — Friday prayer

### Secular Sabbath
- **Digital sabbath** — Screen-free time
- **Work sabbath** — No labor day
- **Silence sabbath** — No speech time
- **Consumption sabbath** — No buying time

### Sabbatical
- **Academic sabbatical** — Research leave
- **Career break** — Extended pause
- **Personal sabbatical** — Life reset time

### Food Fasts
- **Intermittent fasting** — Daily eating windows
- **Multi-day fasting** — Extended abstention
- **Ramadan** — Dawn to dusk fasting
- **Lenten fasting** — 40-day practice

### Digital Fasts
- **Social media fast** — Platform abstention
- **News fast** — Information abstention
- **Screen fast** — Device abstention

### Activity Fasts
- **Speech fast** — Silence practice
- **Travel fast** — Staying put
- **Spending fast** — No purchase time
