# Institutional Seeds: By Type

> Organized social forms where assemblages happen. Structures that you enter.

---

## Formal Education

Credentials, structure, hierarchy. The legitimation machine.

### Universities
- **Research universities** — PhD programs, R1 institutions
- **Liberal arts colleges** — Small, teaching-focused
- **Community colleges** — Open access, practical
- **Extension programs** — Non-degree, continuing education
- **Summer programs** — Intensive, short-term
- **Study abroad** — Displacement as pedagogy

### Professional Schools
- **Law schools** — Adversarial thinking
- **Medical schools** — Body knowledge
- **Business schools** — Optimization logic
- **Architecture schools** — Material + conceptual
- **Divinity schools** — Sacred traditions

### Art/Music Conservatories
- **Juilliard, Curtis, Berklee** — Performance excellence
- **RISD, SAIC, CalArts** — Art + design
- **Film schools (USC, NYU, AFI)** — Narrative craft

### Entry Modes
- Degree enrollment (commitment, credential)
- Audit (presence without stakes)
- Extension/continuing ed (access without admission)
- Online courses (flexibility, reduced presence)
- Visiting/exchange (temporary belonging)

## Workplaces

Economic exchange. Where labor meets compensation.

### Traditional Employment
- **Corporate** — Hierarchy, benefits, career ladder
- **Nonprofit** — Mission-driven, often underpaid
- **Government** — Stability, bureaucracy
- **Academic** — Research + teaching
- **Healthcare** — Care labor, institutional

### Alternative Structures
- **Worker cooperatives** — Shared ownership
- **Employee-owned** — Partial ownership stake
- **Remote-first companies** — Distributed work
- **4-day week companies** — Time experiment

### Freelance/Contract
- **Independent contractor** — Project-based
- **Consultant** — Expertise rental
- **Gig work** — Platform-mediated
- **Portfolio career** — Multiple income streams

### Creative/Craft
- **Studios** — Artistic production
- **Workshops** — Craft production
- **Galleries/agents** — Representation
- **Residencies** — Supported creation time
