# Institutional Seeds: By Function

> What institutions provide — regardless of their type.

---

## Skill Acquisition

Learn to do something. Competence as outcome.

### Hard Skills
- **Technical training programs** — Coding bootcamps, trade schools
- **Instrument lessons** — Music teachers, conservatories
- **Language schools** — Immersion programs, tutors
- **Sports coaching** — Personal trainers, camps
- **Craft apprenticeships** — Woodworking, metalwork, ceramics

### Soft Skills
- **Leadership programs** — Management training
- **Communication workshops** — Public speaking, writing
- **Conflict resolution training** — Mediation certification
- **Facilitation training** — Group process

### Meta-Skills
- **Learning how to learn** — Study skills, metacognition
- **Research methods** — Graduate programs
- **Critical thinking** — Philosophy courses
- **Creative process** — Art schools, writing programs

### Embodied Skills
- **Somatic practices** — Feldenkrais, Alexander Technique
- **Martial arts** — Dojos, training halls
- **Dance training** — Studios, companies
- **Yoga teacher training** — 200/500-hour certifications

## Resource Access

Tools, materials, space. What you cannot afford alone.

### Physical Tools
- **Makerspaces** — 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC
- **Recording studios** — Mixing boards, microphones
- **Darkrooms** — Enlargers, chemicals
- **Woodshops** — Table saws, jointers
- **Ceramics studios** — Kilns, wheels

### Information Resources
- **Research libraries** — Journals, databases
- **Archives** — Primary sources
- **Patent databases** — Technical documentation
- **Legal databases** — Case law, statutes

### Space
- **Rehearsal rooms** — Practice space
- **Co-working spaces** — Office access
- **Studio space** — Art production
- **Land access** — Gardening, farming
- **Kitchen incubators** — Food production

### Capital
- **Grants** — Foundation, government funding
- **Fellowships** — Stipend + time
- **Loans** — Institutional credit
- **Investment** — Venture, angel
- **Crowdfunding platforms** — Collective support

## Credential

Legible proof of capacity. Making invisible visible.

### Academic Credentials
- **Degrees** — BA, MA, PhD, MD, JD
- **Certificates** — Specific skill attestation
- **Transcripts** — Course completion records
- **Honors/awards** — Recognition of excellence

### Professional Credentials
- **Licenses** — Legal authorization to practice
- **Certifications** — Industry-recognized attestation
- **Accreditations** — Organizational approval
- **Board membership** — Professional standing

### Alternative Credentials
- **Portfolio** — Work samples
- **References/testimonials** — Third-party attestation
- **Open badges** — Digital micro-credentials
- **Verified skills** — Platform-based assessment

### Informal Credentials
- **Reputation** — What people say
- **Track record** — History of work
- **Network position** — Who knows you
- **Publications** — Written evidence

## Pack Formation

Find fellow travelers. The tribe you choose.

### Identity-Based Groups
- **Professional associations** — Lawyers, doctors, engineers
- **Alumni networks** — Shared institutional background
- **Affinity groups** — Shared identity (race, gender, sexuality)
- **Diaspora communities** — Shared origin

### Interest-Based Groups
- **Book clubs** — Shared reading
- **Running clubs** — Shared activity
- **Game groups** — Shared play
- **Hobbyist societies** — Shared passion

### Purpose-Based Groups
- **Project teams** — Shared goal
- **Activist groups** — Shared cause
- **Study groups** — Shared learning
- **Support groups** — Shared struggle (AA, grief groups)

### Intensity-Based Groups
- **Cohorts** — Same program, same time
- **Pods** — Small, tight, regular
- **Crews** — Working together repeatedly
- **Platoons** — Bonded through hardship

## Witnessing

Being seen by others. Recognition as function.

### Formal Witnessing
- **Exhibitions/shows** — Art seen publicly
- **Performances** — Witnessed in real-time
- **Publications** — Work made available
- **Presentations** — Ideas shared formally
- **Graduations** — Transitions marked publicly

### Informal Witnessing
- **Open studios** — Work-in-progress seen
- **Rehearsals** — Process witnessed
- **Workshops** — Learning witnessed
- **Documentation** — Work recorded

### Mutual Witnessing
- **Peer groups** — Seeing each other
- **Critique groups** — Structured feedback
- **Accountability partners** — Committed watching
- **Confession/sharing circles** — Vulnerability witnessed

### Institutional Witnessing
- **Reviews** — Formal evaluation
- **Audits** — External assessment
- **Inspections** — Compliance checking
- **Assessments** — Structured measurement

## Rhythm Imposition

External structure that anchors. Time organized from outside.

### Daily Rhythms
- **Work schedules** — 9-5, shifts
- **Class schedules** — Fixed meeting times
- **Opening hours** — When places are accessible
- **Meal times** — Institutional feeding

### Weekly Rhythms
- **Weekly meetings** — Regular assembly
- **Sabbath/day off** — Weekly punctuation
- **Weekly classes** — Recurring education
- **Deadlines** — Regular pressure points

### Seasonal Rhythms
- **Academic calendar** — Semesters, breaks
- **Fiscal year** — Budgeting cycles
- **Conference seasons** — Annual gatherings
- **Holiday schedules** — Cultural punctuation

### Life Rhythms
- **Milestones** — Graduations, promotions
- **Reviews** — Annual evaluation
- **Sabbaticals** — Periodic renewal
- **Tenure clocks** — Up-or-out timing

### Intensive Rhythms
- **Retreats** — Concentrated practice
- **Residencies** — Extended immersion
- **Bootcamps** — Compressed learning
- **Sprints** — Focused bursts

## Scaffolding

Temporary support structures that enable development.

### Educational Scaffolding
- **Prerequisites** — Staged skill building
- **Tutorials** — Guided practice
- **Office hours** — On-demand support
- **Mentorship** — Developmental relationship

### Creative Scaffolding
- **Prompts/assignments** — Structure for generation
- **Constraints** — Productive limitation
- **Templates** — Starting structures
- **Feedback loops** — Iterative refinement

### Professional Scaffolding
- **Internships** — Protected entry
- **Residencies** — Supported transition
- **Fellowships** — Development funding
- **Incubators/accelerators** — Startup support

### Personal Scaffolding
- **Therapy** — Psychological support
- **Coaching** — Goal pursuit support
- **Spiritual direction** — Contemplative guidance
- **12-step programs** — Recovery structure

---

*What function do you need? What is missing? Institutions provide these functions — sometimes well, sometimes poorly. Knowing what function you seek helps you find (or build) the right institution.*
