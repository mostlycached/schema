# Material Seeds: By Relation to Body

> How does the object meet the body?

## Worn

Clothing, jewelry, prosthetics. On the body.

### First Layer
- **Undergarments** — Intimate support
- **Socks** — Foot comfort
- **Base layers** — Temperature regulation

### Main Layer
- **Shirts/tops** — Upper body cover
- **Pants/skirts** — Lower body cover
- **Dresses** — Full body cover

### Outer Layer
- **Jackets/coats** — Weather protection
- **Rainwear** — Water protection
- **Ceremonial robes** — Sacred covering

### Extremities
- **Shoes** — Foot interface with ground
- **Gloves** — Hand protection
- **Hats** — Head covering
- **Scarves** — Neck warmth

### Adornment
- **Rings** — Finger marking
- **Necklaces** — Neck adornment
- **Earrings** — Ear marking
- **Watches** — Wrist time

### Prosthetics/Extensions
- **Glasses/contacts** — Vision extension
- **Hearing aids** — Auditory extension
- **Mobility aids** — Movement extension
