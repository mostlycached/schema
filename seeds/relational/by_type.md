# Relational Seeds: By Type

> Connections to others. What form does the relation take?

---

## Dyad

One-to-one. The pair.

### Intimate Dyads
- **Romantic partner** — Love relation
- **Best friend** — Deep friendship
- **Sibling** — Blood bond
- **Parent-child** — Generational bond

### Functional Dyads
- **Mentor-mentee** — Transmission relation
- **Therapist-client** — Healing relation
- **Teacher-student** — Educational relation
- **Coach-athlete** — Training relation

### Creative Dyads
- **Collaborator** — Co-creation partner
- **Accountability partner** — Mutual support
- **Writing partner** — Parallel creation
- **Practice partner** — Skill development

---

## Pack

Small group, functional. The working unit.

### Creative Packs
- **Band** — Musical unit
- **Ensemble** — Performance unit
- **Writing group** — Literary unit
- **Studio collective** — Art unit

### Work Packs
- **Team** — Project unit
- **Squad** — Agile unit
- **Crew** — Production unit
- **Partnership** — Business unit

### Support Packs
- **Support group** — Recovery unit
- **Pod** — Close circle
- **Cohort** — Learning unit
- **Cell** — Activist unit

---

## Collective

Larger group, identity-based. Shared belonging.

### Professional Collectives
- **Association** — Field belonging
- **Union** — Labor belonging
- **Guild** — Craft belonging

### Identity Collectives
- **Community** — Place/identity belonging
- **Movement** — Cause belonging
- **Scene** — Cultural belonging

### Intentional Collectives
- **Commune** — Living arrangement
- **Cooperative** — Economic arrangement
- **Network** — Loose affiliation

---

## Crowd

Anonymous mass. The many.

### Physical Crowds
- **Concert audience** — Music gathering
- **Protest crowd** — Political gathering
- **Festival crowd** — Celebration gathering
- **Sports crowd** — Athletic gathering

### Virtual Crowds
- **Social media following** — Online mass
- **Stream audience** — Live digital gathering
- **Forum membership** — Topical mass

---

## Audience

Those who witness. Recipients of expression.

### Live Audience
- **Concert-goers** — Music witnesses
- **Theater audience** — Performance witnesses
- **Lecture attendees** — Ideas witnesses

### Mediated Audience
- **Readers** — Text witnesses
- **Viewers** — Visual witnesses
- **Listeners** — Audio witnesses

---

## Antagonist

Those who oppose. Productive friction.

### Personal Antagonists
- **Rival** — Competitive other
- **Critic** — Evaluating other
- **Enemy** — Hostile other

### Structural Antagonists
- **Opponent** — Game/debate other
- **Competition** — Market other
- **Opposition** — Political other

---

## Ghost

Those no longer present. Absence that affects.

### The Dead
- **Deceased loved ones** — Personal dead
- **Ancestors** — Generational dead
- **Historical figures** — Influential dead

### The Absent
- **Lost friends** — Disappeared relations
- **Ex-partners** — Past intimacy
- **Estranged family** — Broken bonds

---

## Figure

Idealized other. Model, exemplar.

### Personal Figures
- **Role model** — Someone to emulate
- **Hero** — Someone to admire
- **Muse** — Someone who inspires

### Cultural Figures
- **Icons** — Cultural exemplars
- **Saints** — Religious exemplars
- **Masters** — Craft exemplars
