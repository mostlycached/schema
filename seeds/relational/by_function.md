# Relational Seeds: By Function

> What do these relations provide?

---

## Witness

Being seen. Recognition as gift.

### Formal Witnessing
- **Audience** — Work witnessed publicly
- **Jury/committee** — Work evaluated
- **Congregation** — Practice witnessed

### Mutual Witnessing
- **Peer group** — Seeing each other
- **Critical friends** — Supportive feedback
- **Accountability partners** — Tracking each other

### Professional Witnessing
- **Therapist** — Psychological witness
- **Spiritual director** — Soul witness
- **Coach** — Development witness

---

## Collaborator

Shared production. Making together.

### Creative Collaboration
- **Co-author** — Writing together
- **Bandmates** — Playing together
- **Co-founder** — Building together

### Work Collaboration
- **Project team** — Delivering together
- **Research team** — Discovering together
- **Committee** — Deciding together

---

## Interlocutor

Thinking together. Dialogue as method.

### Intellectual
- **Discussion partner** — Ideas exchange
- **Debate partner** — Position testing
- **Study partner** — Learning together

### Personal
- **Confidant** — Private sharing
- **Advisor** — Guidance seeking
- **Sounding board** — Idea testing

---

## Teacher

Transmission. Knowledge flowing.

### Formal Teachers
- **Professor** — Academic instruction
- **Instructor** — Skill teaching
- **Master** — Craft transmission

### Informal Teachers
- **Mentor** — Life guidance
- **Elder** — Wisdom sharing
- **Guide** — Path showing

---

## Student

Receiving transmission. Learning from.

### Formal Student
- **Enrolled student** — Structured learning
- **Apprentice** — Craft learning
- **Disciple** — Practice learning

### Informal Student
- **Mentee** — Receiving guidance
- **Follower** — Learning from model
- **Devotee** — Spiritual learning

---

## Care

Support, maintenance. Holding each other.

### Receiving Care
- **Being parented** — Childhood care
- **Being nursed** — Health care
- **Being supported** — Crisis care

### Giving Care
- **Parenting** — Child care
- **Nursing** — Health giving
- **Supporting friends** — Mutual aid

### Mutual Care
- **Partnership** — Reciprocal support
- **Friendship** — Mutual holding
- **Community** — Collective support

---

## Antagonism

Productive friction. Opposition that sharpens.

### Constructive
- **Sparring partner** — Safe opposition
- **Devil's advocate** — Position challenge
- **Competitor** — Performance driving

### Uncomfortable Growth
- **Critic** — Hard feedback
- **Rival** — Achievement pressure
- **Challenger** — Assumption questioning

---

## Play

Non-purposive encounter. Relation for its own sake.

### Structured Play
- **Game partners** — Rule-bound play
- **Sports teammates** — Athletic play
- **Improvisers** — Creative play

### Unstructured Play
- **Hanging out** — Being together
- **Fooling around** — Spontaneous play
- **Flirtation** — Romantic play

---

*Functions can overlap. One person may serve multiple functions. What functions do you need filled?*
