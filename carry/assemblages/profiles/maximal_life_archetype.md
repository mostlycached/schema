## 2. Profile: The "Maximal" Archetype
*Instead of a list of 70 disconnected items, the Maximal Life is organized around 4-5 "Attractors" or "Engines" that generate all other behavior.*

### Engine I: The Work (The Fire)
*Not a "job," but an Oeuvre. The central organizing principle of attention.*
*   **The Project**: A multi-decade arc of inquiry (e.g., "Mapping the Topology of Grief" or "Building the Symbiotic City").
*   **Mode**: The distinction between "labor" and "leisure" has collapsed. Reading Didi-Huberman is not "distraction"; it is fuel for the code.
*   **Animism**: **Fire** (Transformation). Everything encountered is consumed and transformed into the Work.

### Engine II: The Ground (The Earth)
*The non-negotiable bodily anchor that permits high-altitude thinking.*
*   **The Practice**: A rigorous, repetitive somatic engagement (e.g., Ashtanga, long-distance swimming, cello).
*   **Function**: It is not "exercise" for health; it is **tuning**. It teaches the nervous system how to handle duration, resistance, and pain, which is then applied to The Work.
*   **Animism**: **Earth** / **Gravity**. Returning to the floor, to weight, to the real.

### Engine III: The Council (The Relation)
*Relationships as "Plots of Land" rather than "Networking Nodes."*
*   **The Band**: A small, tight clusters of 3-5 peers who are "co-conspirators." Communication is continuous, high-bandwidth, and jargon-dense (a private language).
*   **Function**: Externalized cognition. You think *through* them. They provide the "resistance" needed to sharpen the Knife.
*   **Animism**: **Sea** (Merging) + **Knife** (Critique).

### Engine IV: The Sanctuary (The Space)
*The environment as an externalized nervous system.*
*   **The Room**: Not "decorated," but **arranged**. Every object (the serres book, the specific teapot, the quality of light) is an "affordance" placed to trigger specific states of mind.
*   **Function**: The room "remembers" for you. You enter the chair, and the "Writer" sub-routine loads instantly.
*   **Animism**: **Night** (The Surround).

## 3. The "Seeds" in Bloom
In the Maximal Life, the "Seeds" (from `SEEDS.md`) have not just been encountered; they have successfully **cracked the Wounds** and restructured the subject.

*   **The Wound Cracked**: "Someone else's concept of time" (Temporal Wound).
    *   **The Result**: The Maximal subject lives in "Aeon" (unlimited time of the event) rather than "Chronos" (measured time). They are never "busy"; they are simply varying in intensity.
*   **The Wound Cracked**: "The body-as-boundary" (Spatial Wound).
    *   **The Result**: The "Porous Exchange." They feel the cold air not as an assault, but as a texture. They feel the room as an extension of their lungs.

## 4. Why It Looks "Maximal"
It appears maximal not because it has *more stuff* (Maya likely owns more objects and has more apps), but because it has **more reality**.
*   **Density**: Every action has multiple layers of meaning. Cooking dinner is not a chore; it is an improvisation practice (Fire) and a care ritual for The Council (Link).
*   **Resonance**: A setback in The Work resonates with The Practice. A blockage in writing is solved by a breakthrough in running.

## Summary Definition
> **A Maximal Life is a life where the "Container" (System) is no longer distinct from the "Content" (Self). The subject has become the World.**
