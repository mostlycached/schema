# MAP-Elites: Ordinary Lives

*A 2D matrix for systematic navigation of human experience*

## Dimensions

- **X-Axis: Labor Type** (Hands-on/Material ↔ Symbolic/Informational)
- **Y-Axis: Stability** (Routine/Stable ↔ Precarious/Crisis)

---

## THE MAP (4x4)

|  | **Primary/Extraction** | **Processing/Service** | **Technical/Specialist** | **Symbolic/Clerical** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stable/Secure** | [N1] Farmer (established) | [N2] Factory Line Worker (union) | [N3] Licensed Electrician | [N4] Government Clerk |

## NICHE DEFINITIONS

### N1: Stable Material-Primary (The Established Farmer)
- **Logic**: Seasonal rhythm, land-relation, multi-generational knowledge.
- **Traits**: `Cyclical-Planner`, `Weather-Reader`, `Equipment-Maintainer`.

### N2: Stable Material-Processing (The Union Line Worker)
- **Logic**: Repetition, collective bargaining, shift-structure.
- **Traits**: `Paced-Operator`, `Safety-Protocol-Follower`, `Break-Time-Ritualist`.

### N3: Stable Technical (The Licensed Electrician)
- **Logic**: Certification, code-adherence, diagnostic intuition.
- **Traits**: `Systematic-Troubleshooter`, `Code-Complier`, `Risk-Assessor`.

### N4: Stable Symbolic (The Government Clerk)
- **Logic**: Procedure, queue-management, form-processing.
- **Traits**: `Queue-Manager`, `Regulation-Applier`, `Case-File-Navigator`.

### N5: Routine Material-Primary (The Ranch Hand)
- **Logic**: Animal-care, dawn-to-dusk labor, weather-dependence.
- **Traits**: `Animal-Handler`, `Fence-Mender`, `Early-Riser`.

### N6: Routine Material-Service (The Short-Order Cook)
- **Logic**: Speed, memorization, heat-tolerance.
- **Traits**: `Order-Ticker-Reader`, `Mise-en-Place-Practitioner`, `Grease-Fire-Preventer`.

### N7: Routine Technical (The Auto Mechanic)
- **Logic**: Diagnosis, parts-sourcing, customer-explanation.
- **Traits**: `Engine-Listener`, `Parts-Catalog-Navigator`, `Trust-Builder`.

### N8: Routine Symbolic (The Data Entry Worker)
- **Logic**: Speed, accuracy, screen-focus.
- **Traits**: `Keystroke-Optimizer`, `Error-Corrector`, `Posture-Maintainer`.

### N9: Transitional Material-Primary (The Seasonal Migrant Worker)
- **Logic**: Following the harvest, temporary housing, remittance.
- **Traits**: `Crop-Follower`, `Transient-Community-Builder`, `Money-Sender`.

### N10: Transitional Material-Service (The Home Health Aide)
- **Logic**: Intimate care, emotional labor, low wage.
- **Traits**: `Body-Lifter`, `Dignity-Preserver`, `Boundary-Negotiator`.

### N11: Transitional Technical (The Apprentice Plumber)
- **Logic**: Learning-on-the-job, hierarchy, tool-familiarity.
- **Traits**: `Tool-Carrier`, `Instruction-Follower`, `Mistake-Learner`.

### N12: Transitional Symbolic (The Temp Agency Worker)
- **Logic**: Assignment-hopping, no benefits, constant adaptation.
- **Traits**: `Quick-Study`, `Disposability-Aware`, `Policy-Ignorance-Navigator`.

### N13: Precarious Material-Primary (The Unemployed Miner)
- **Logic**: Industry collapse, identity-loss, retraining pressure.
- **Traits**: `Skill-Without-Market`, `Community-Mourner`, `Benefit-Navigator`.

### N14: Precarious Material-Service (The Gig Delivery Driver)
- **Logic**: App-dependence, no floor, vehicle-as-workplace.
- **Traits**: `Rating-Maintainer`, `Route-Optimizer`, `Tip-Hoper`.

### N15: Precarious Technical (The Uninsured Trucker)
- **Logic**: Long-haul solitude, health risks, owner-operator debt.
- **Traits**: `Mile-Counter`, `Caffeine-Dependent`, `Debt-Carrier`.

### N16: Precarious Symbolic (The Incarcerated Clerk)
- **Logic**: Forced labor, limited agency, time-serving.
- **Traits**: `Supervised-Worker`, `Time-Marker`, `System-Navigator`.
